About Us

Adam and Eddie are both undergraduates at
the University of California, San Diego.

How we got started

Back in 2016, we met at Qualcomm's annual DECA Conference and became close friends. Soon after getting our offers from Qualcomm, we decided to become roommates. We started this project as a way to protect the millions of bikers out there who want to be able to roam the streets, without being prone to accidents. Our main priority is to create products that protect our customers and to make sure that they feel safe while on the road. We are committed to making quality products and always put our customers before anything else.

Smart Helmet

SnapCruze was created with the vision of keeping riders everywhere safe. Whether you ride a bike or motorcycle, our smart helmet allows everybody to roam the streets with more confidence than ever before.

+

Safety First

+

Our smart helmet comes fully equipped with sensors to help you avoid unseen obstacles and increase your visibility to other drivers on the road.

+

Blind Spot Detection

+

Two rear-mounted proximity sensors will alert you of other vehicles in your blindspots + via the front LCD.

+

Connecting Your World

+

SnapCruze connects with your smartphone to analyze your surroundings and help + you to find safer routes to your favorite destinations.

+

Automatic Brakelight

+

Our on-board accelerometer detects when you are slowing down to stop and toggles a brakelight on the rear of the helmet.

+

You In The Driver's Seat

+

Our smartphone companion app allows you to completely customize the SnapCruze helmet's notification settings. Your safety never takes the backseat!

+

Customizable LED Headlight

+

Our bright LED headlight not only acts as a forward facing spotlight,the LED design is customizable via the SnapCruze app too!

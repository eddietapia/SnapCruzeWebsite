About Us

Adam and Eddie are both undergraduates at
the University of California, San Diego.

How we got started

Back in 2016, we met at Qualcomm's annual DECA Conference and became close friends. Soon after getting our offers from Qualcomm, we decided to become roommates. We started this project as a way to protect the millions of bikers out there who want to be able to roam the streets, without being prone to accidents. Our main priority is to create products that protect our customers and to make sure that they feel safe while on the road. We are committed to making quality products and always put our customers before anything else.
